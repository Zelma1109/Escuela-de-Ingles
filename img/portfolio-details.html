The ETS Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL®) exam measures your ability to use and understand English at the university level. In addition, it assesses how you combine your listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills to carry out academic tasks.

    *Total Score: (0-100 pts.)

The Proulex English Proficiency Test (EPT) is a test that measures your ability to use English proficiency at the intermediate level. The test is designed to meet international standards and is aligned with the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR).

The Cambridge English Teaching Knowledge Test (TKT) exam shows how you are developing as a teacher. It is ideal for people who want to demonstrate their teaching knowledge with a globally recognized certificate.
